ABOUT ME

Joe Klein is a former television producer, animator and digital artist turned software engineer, based out of Jersey City, NJ.

Coming into programming with a visual background, he is uniquely suited to building projects that are not only functional, but aesthetic, as well.

On your average day, he can be found coding his latest passion project, sketching the latest idea that popped in his head, or hunched over his Wacom table animating absurd cartoons.
